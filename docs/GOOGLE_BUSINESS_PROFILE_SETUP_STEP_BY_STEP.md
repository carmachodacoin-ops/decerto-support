# Google Business Profile – Step-by-Step Setup Guide  
## After Removing Your Previous Account

Use this guide when you’ve removed an old GBP and want to create or manage a profile with the **correct** Google account.

**Last updated:** March 2025  

---

## Before you start

- [ ] You’ve **removed** the old Business Profile (wrong email) as needed.
- [ ] You’re ready to use the **correct Google account** (the email you want to manage the profile with).
- [ ] You have: **business name**, **country**, **category**, **location or service area**, **phone**, **website URL**.

---

## Part 1: Use the correct Google account

### Step 1.1 – Sign out of the wrong account (if needed)

1. On Google (e.g. [google.com](https://www.google.com)), click your **profile picture or initial** (top right).
2. Click **“Sign out”** (or “Sign out of all accounts” if you have several).
3. Close the tab or go to [business.google.com](https://business.google.com) in a **new** tab.

### Step 1.2 – Sign in with the correct account

1. Go to **[business.google.com](https://business.google.com)**.
2. Click **“Sign in”** (or “Manage now”).
3. Enter the **email and password** of the account you want to use (e.g. your company email).
4. Complete sign-in (2-step verification if prompted).

From here on, everything you do will be with this account.

---

## Part 2: Create a new Business Profile

### Step 2.1 – Open the creation flow

1. At [business.google.com](https://business.google.com), click **“Add your business to Google”** or **“Create a Business Profile”** (wording may vary).
2. If you see a list of businesses, click **“Add business”** or **“Create new profile”**.

### Step 2.2 – Enter business name and category

1. **Business name:** Type the exact name (e.g. **Decerto Supports** or **NAC Tech Solution**).
2. Click **Next**.
3. **Category:** Choose **one** primary category (e.g. “Disability services and support organisation”, “Technology company”, or closest match). Start typing to search.
4. Click **Next**.

### Step 2.3 – Choose location type

1. **Do you have a location customers can visit?**
   - **Yes** → Enter full **business address** (street, city, state, postcode). Click **Next**.
   - **No / I serve customers at their location** → Select that option. You’ll add **service areas** (e.g. Perth, Thimphu) next. Click **Next**.

### Step 2.4 – Add service area (if you chose “serve customers at their location”)

1. Enter **region, city, or area** (e.g. Perth WA, Thimphu).
2. You can add multiple areas. Click **Next** when done.

### Step 2.5 – Add contact details

1. **Phone:** Enter the business phone number.
2. **Website:** Enter the full URL (e.g. `https://decerto-support.vercel.app` or your live domain).
3. Click **Next**.

### Step 2.6 – Verify ownership

1. Google will ask you to **verify** you own or represent the business.
2. Choose the method offered:
   - **Postcard** – Code sent to your business address (often 5–14 days). Enter address if needed.
   - **Phone** – Call or SMS with a code (instant, if available).
   - **Email** – Code to your business email (if available).
3. Submit the request. If postcard: wait for it, then enter the code in the verification page.
4. After the code is accepted, your profile is **verified** and can go live.

---

## Part 3: Complete and test your profile

### Step 3.1 – Add more info (optional but recommended)

1. From the dashboard, click **“Edit profile”** (or open the profile).
2. Fill in:
   - **Description** (short, with main keywords).
   - **Opening hours** (or “By appointment”).
   - **Logo** and **photos** (if you have them).
   - **Services** (list main services).
3. Save changes.

### Step 3.2 – Confirm which email is managing the profile

1. Click your **profile picture or initial** (top right) on [business.google.com](https://business.google.com).
2. The **email** shown there is the one managing this Business Profile. Note it for your records.

### Step 3.3 – Test the website link

1. In Google Search, search for your **business name** (e.g. “Decerto Supports” or “NAC Tech Solution”).
2. In the knowledge panel (right side), click **“Website”**.
3. Confirm it opens the URL you added (e.g. your test or live site).

---

## Part 4: If you manage multiple businesses or clients

- **One profile per business:** Each real business gets **one** GBP. Use the correct Google account for each (e.g. client’s account for client’s business, your account for your own business).
- **Add other people:** In the profile: **Users** (or **People**) → **Add users** → invite by email (Owner or Manager). They’ll manage the same profile.
- **Your own dev business:** Create one GBP for your company (e.g. NAC Tech Solution) with **your** correct email; use it for testing and linking to test sites or your portfolio.

## Quick checklist (after removing the old account)

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Sign out of the wrong Google account (if needed). |
| 2 | Go to [business.google.com](https://business.google.com) and sign in with the **correct** account. |
| 3 | Click **Add your business** / **Create a Business Profile**. |
| 4 | Enter business name → category → location or service area → phone → website. |
| 5 | Complete **verification** (postcard / phone / email). |
| 6 | Edit profile: add description, hours, logo/photos, services. |
| 7 | Confirm email: click your profile icon (top right) to see which account manages the profile. |
| 8 | Test: search your business name and click **Website** to confirm it opens your site. |

---

## Need to remove a profile again?

1. Go to [business.google.com](https://business.google.com) and sign in with the **account that owns** the profile.
2. Select the business → open the **three-dot menu (⋮)** (e.g. next to “Profile strength” or the business name).
3. Choose **“Remove Business Profile”** → **“Remove profile content and managers”** and confirm.
