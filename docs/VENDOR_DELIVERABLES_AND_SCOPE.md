## 1. Parties to This Contract

| Party | Role | Description |
|-------|------|-------------|
| **You (Website Owner)** | Client | The party that owns the Decerto Supports website, provides feedback on drafts, and pays for the work. |
| **I (Developer)** | Contractor / Vendor | The party that designs, builds, and delivers the website, logo drafts, and advertising as set out in this contract. |

This contract sets out:

## 4. Website Draft Review – 3 Rounds

**I** will provide the website in **three (3) formal drafts**. **You** will review each draft and give written feedback.

| Round | I provide | You provide |
|-------|-----------|-------------|
| **Draft 1** | First full build (structure, content, design, responsiveness) | Written feedback within agreed time |
| **Draft 2** | Revised version incorporating Draft 1 feedback | Written feedback within agreed time |
| **Draft 3** | Final version incorporating Draft 2 feedback | Sign-off for launch or list of small fixes |

- All feedback from **you** must be in writing (email or shared document).
- Revisions are limited to the agreed scope. Changes that go beyond this scope after Draft 3 may be charged separately (see **Section 9**).

---

## 5. Facebook & Instagram Advertising

- **I** will set up and deliver **ads on Facebook and Instagram** as part of this contract.
- **Deliverables** include: ad creative (copy and/or visuals), targeting setup, and campaign structure as agreed with **you**.
- Campaign goals, budget, duration, and number of ad variants may be set out in a separate brief or addendum we both agree on.

---

## 6. Website Logo Draft Review – 3 Rounds

**I** will provide the **website logo** (or brand logo for the website) in **three (3) draft rounds**. **You** will review and give written feedback.

| Round | I provide | You provide |
|-------|-----------|-------------|
| **Logo Draft 1** | Initial logo concepts or first draft | Written feedback |
| **Logo Draft 2** | Revised logo based on your feedback | Written feedback |
| **Logo Draft 3** | Final logo draft | Sign-off or minor refinements |

- Feedback from **you** must be in writing.
- The final agreed logo will be used on the website and, where agreed, in advertising and other materials.

---

## 7. Website Workflow (Brief Description)

A brief description of how the Decerto Supports website is intended to work (for reference in this contract):

1. Users visit the site (homepage or landing pages).
2. **Navigation:** Home, Services, Careers, Contact, and Settings.
3. **Content:** Disability services, approach, testimonials, blogs, and contact options.
4. **Accessibility & preferences:** A Settings area lets users change **text size** (zoom) and **page background** (default, warm, cool, light, dark). Choices are saved (e.g. in the browser).
5. **Contact:** Contact details, contact form, phone, and email.
6. **Careers:** Vacancies and application form (e.g. with resume upload).
7. **Responsive design:** The site works on desktop, tablet, and mobile.
8. **Trust & compliance:** Content reflects disability support context and Australian audience (e.g. geo, locale, accessibility).

Technical details (hosting, deployment, CMS if any) to be agreed between you and me.
